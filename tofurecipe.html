Good tofu recipe

Technically makes 2 servings but I tend to eat the whole lot in a big bowl

Ingredients

  • 280g tofu (I used Tofoo because it's my faaaaave so the 280g is just the size of one pack of Tofoo but it seems to work!)
  • 1/3 - 2/3 cup* nutritional yeast
  • 2-4 dessertspoons* of olive oil
  • 2-4 dessertspoons* of soy sauce
  • As much garlic as you can be bothered to mince

Method

  1. Preheat your oven to 200F
  2. Tear the tofu up into uneven bits (which is what I do to save washing up an extra knife but like feel free to chop it I can't stop you)
  3. Put all the ingredients except tofu into a big bowl and stir it up until it makes a flavour squidge
  4. Add the tofu and stir until all the tofu is covered by the flavour squidge
  5. Put on a baking tray and stick in the oven for 15 minutes
  6. Take out and turn all the pieces over so every side of the tofu gets super crunchy
  7. Put back in the oven for 15 more minutes

Serving suggestion: the bottom of the baking tray is probably covered in salty crunchy goodness so don't be ashamed to scrape it off and eat it, it's really good go for it


So I used to use the lower amount of flavour ingredients for half a pack of tofu but then I didn't have much nutritional yeast left so I used half the amount of flavour ingredients on a whole pack of tofu and it was still super good so go with your gut on amounts.
