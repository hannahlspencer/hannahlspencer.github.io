BBC News Apps

Mar - Sep '19

I joined News apps in a period of transformation in which both the app appearance and structure was being completely scrapped and rewritten to better serve the diverse needs of its 9 million weekly users. I was able to contribute from the project's very beginning, from design sprints with the UX team, to user research, to deciding on ways to implement and test the new systems.
